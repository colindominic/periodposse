About

Thank you for joining the Posse – we are so excited you are here.

The Period Posse is a community action group dedicated to developing equitable access to menstrual products, at both a national and international level.

We believe in teamwork, collaboration, and grassroots efforts.

We believe in menstrual equity, where access to menstrual products is a right. Period.

We believe that providing menstrual products to those in need is one method of providing choice, security and comfort – which can have an incredible ripple effect.

Values

Inclusive Language

  • People who do not identify as women menstruate. We use the term “menstruating people” or “menstruating folk” to refer to people who use menstrual products.

  • We use the term “menstrual products” over “feminine hygiene products” (and its variants).

Community Based Efforts

  • We believe in engagement and asking organization about their needs – and finding innovative methods to engage our community.

Complex Problem

  • We believe that the issue of menstrual product equity is complex – commodification and commercialization of the female body, financial misogyny, period stigma, poverty, and homelessness intersect and converge to create this incredible issue for people.
